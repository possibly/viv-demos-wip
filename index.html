viv demos

Interactive simulations built on the viv runtime.

  • 01
    Hello World
    Three characters in a tavern who greet each other. Set a seed, run, and scrub through the timeline.
  • mmo
    MMO Starter World
    A lone adventurer — randomly assigned a race, class, and name — wanders through five subzones of a fantasy starter region.
  • pw
    Promweek
    Five characters in two acts. Click an initiator and a receiver — only the actions that initiator is willing to take show up. Goals describe states between characters, not yours.
