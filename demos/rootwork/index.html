Rootwork

A garden game. Plants run plans. Conditions don't care who satisfies them.

Season · Day 0

You tend four plots. Plants are characters with multi-phase life plans. Each phase advances when soil/weather conditions are satisfied — and the conditions don't care whether you satisfied them, or the rain, or a companion plant, or a wandering bee.

Every action — yours and the world's — gets recorded in the chronicle. At season's end, the chronicle is sifted: did you stay close, or let nature do the work?
